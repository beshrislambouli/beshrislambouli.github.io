I am a senior undergraduate at MIT studying Computer Science and Engineering. My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and computer systems. Advised by [Prof. Yoon Kim](https://people.csail.mit.edu/yoonkim/), my current research focuses on building smarter and faster algorithms and systems for large language models and their applications.

Some of my current and past affiliations include [MIT](https://www.mit.edu/), [CSAIL](https://www.csail.mit.edu/), [Jump Trading](https://www.jumptrading.com/), [ZeroRISC](https://www.zerorisc.com/blog/zeroriscs-summer-interns-expand-runtime-flexibility-of-our-secureos), [MIT Parallel and Distributed Operating Systems Group](https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/), [Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard](https://www.broadinstitute.org/), [Battlecode](https://battlecode.org/), [International Olympiad in Informatics](https://stats.ioinformatics.org/people/7281), [Asia-Pacific Informatics Olympiad](https://apio2020.toki.id/results), [Codeforces](https://codeforces.com/profile/Beshest.?locale=en), and [Delta Tau Delta](https://delts.mit.edu/).
